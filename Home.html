About Us

Welcome to White Pearl Dental Clinic, where your oral health and comfort are our top priorities. With a team of dedicated and experienced dental professionals, we provide exceptional care in a warm and friendly environment. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure accurate diagnoses and the most effective treatments. At Mouth Dental Clinic, we are committed to enhancing your smile and overall well-being. Whether you need a routine check-up, cosmetic dentistry, or specialized treatments, we are here to cater to all your dental needs. Your journey to a healthy, beautiful smile begins with us. Trust us with your oral health, and let us give you a reason to smile confidently.




Services

  • Restorative Dentistry – placement of cosmetic restorations, general dental cavity restoration, Restoring badly broken
    down teeth or teeth damaged due to trauma, replacement of black fillings with white (only white
    fillings placed/metal(amalgum) not used).
  • Oral Surgery – Removal of teeth, surgical removal of impacted teeth.
  • Implantology – placement of implants. Straumann, Nobel and Dentium, immeadiate implant placement:treatment
    completed within one week.
  • Endodontics – Root Canal treatment for teeth which cannot be saved other than for extraction.
  • Prosthodontics – Porcelain/Metal crowns and bridgework, acrylic and metal Dentures, porcelain/ceramic veneer
    placement to correct cosmetics or misaligned, protruded teeth.
  • Periodontology – Tooth scaling, polishing, stain removal, and oral hygiene instructions and follow up.,
    gum treatment, synthetic bone grafting.
  • Pediatric Dentistry – Care for toddlers and children and adolescent teeth.
  • Geriatric Dentistry – Care for over 70s dental needs.
  • Orthodontics – Correction of crooked or/and protruded teeth by use of fixed appliances/removable appliances.
  • General Dental Practice
  • Cosmetic Tooth whitening – In office whitening within 1 hour, 4 shades whiter teeth !
  • Community Dentistry – Fluoride application, recalcifying treatment, Prevention of caries and gum disease,
    creating awareness on dental diseases and treatment available.
  • Dental radiology – Intraoral peri apical x ray available.
