Services

  • Restorative Dentistry – placement of cosmetic restorations, general dental cavity restoration, Restoring badly broken down teeth or teeth damaged due to trauma, replacement of black fillings with white (only white fillings placed/metal(amalgum) not used).
  • Oral Surgery – Removal of teeth, surgical removal of impacted teeth.
  • Implantology – placement of implants. Straumann, Nobel and Dentium, immeadiate implant placement:treatment completed within one week.
  • Endodontics – Root Canal treatment for teeth which cannot be saved other than for extraction.
  • Prosthodontics – Porcelain/Metal crowns and bridgework, acrylic and metal Dentures, porcelain/ceramic veneer placement to correct cosmetics or misaligned, protruded teeth.
  • Periodontology – Tooth scaling, polishing, stain removal, and oral hygiene instructions and follow up., gum treatment, synthetic bone grafting.
  • Pediatric Dentistry – Care for toddlers and children and adolescent teeth.
  • Geriatric Dentistry – Care for over 70s dental needs.
  • Orthodontics – Correction of crooked or/and protruded teeth by use of fixed appliances/removable appliances.
  • General Dental Practice
  • Cosmetic Tooth whitening – In office whitening within 1 hour, 4 shades whiter teeth !
  • Community Dentistry – Fluoride application, recalcifying treatment, Prevention of caries and gum disease, creating awareness on dental diseases and treatment available.
  • Dental radiology – Intraoral peri apical x ray available.
